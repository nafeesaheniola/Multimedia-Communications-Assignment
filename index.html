GRADUATING INTO UNCERTAINTY: THE FEAR BEHIND EVERY DEGREE

INTRODUCTION

Graduation is often seen as a moment of joy, celebration, and achievement. It represents years of hard work, sleepless nights, assignments, and exams finally paying off. However, behind the smiles and photos lies a hidden emotion that many graduates do not openly discuss—fear. In today’s world, graduating does not automatically guarantee employment or financial stability. Many graduates step out of school into a world filled with uncertainty, competition, and pressure to succeed immediately. This reality creates anxiety, confusion, and fear about the future.

THE REALITY AFTER GRADUATION

Graduation is often seen as a celebration, but for many students, it brings fear and uncertainty. The question “what next?” becomes louder than the applause.Many graduates expects jobs shortly after school,but the reality is different.
